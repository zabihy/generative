# ψ–Θ–χ Generative Framework

This page hosts a collection of technical preprints developing a unified
generative framework for physics based on the ψ–Θ–χ continuum.
The program investigates how spacetime, geometry, fields, and measurement
emerge from a common generative architecture, culminating in a kernel-based
model of physical law and quantum measurement.

The works are published as independent preprints on Zenodo and are intended
to be read as a coherent sequence.

---

## Related Works

This article belongs to a broader theoretical program investigating how
spacetime, geometry, and field phenomena emerge from the ψ–Θ–χ continuum.
Earlier works in this series develop successive layers of the framework—from
foundational vacuum structure to kernel execution models and quantum
measurement dynamics.
